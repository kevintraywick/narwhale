Tier 1 — Must Have
F1Revenue ModelPresent
Crystal clear: 10% commission on each transaction. $80/night average room price × 10% = ~$8 per night. With average 3-night stays, ~$25 per booking. Simple, marketplace-standard.
F2Monthly Burn RateAbsent
Not stated. Three cofounders in San Francisco with a web platform — monthly costs are inferable but should be explicit. Even at angel stage, investors need to know how fast the clock is ticking.
F3Use of FundsAbsent
$500K ask with zero breakdown. Engineering? Marketing? Events? Craigslist integration? Hiring? The deck asks for money but doesn't say where it goes.
F4Runway CalculationPartial
"12 months financing" implies 12 months of runway from the $500K raise, but without a burn rate this is an assertion not a calculation.
Tier 2 — Should Have
F53-Year P&L ProjectionAbsent
Only a 12-month revenue target ($2M from 80K transactions). No expense projections, no multi-year view, no path to profitability timeline.
F6Unit Economics (CAC/LTV)Partial
Revenue per transaction is clear ($25 avg fee). But no customer acquisition cost — how much does it cost to get a host to list? A guest to book? No LTV or retention data.
F7Gross Margin TrajectoryAbsent
Not discussed. At 10% take rate, payment processing alone eats 2-3%. What's left after hosting costs, customer support, and fraud/chargebacks?
F812-Month Cash FlowAbsent
No monthly cash flow model. The 80K transaction / $2M target is a year-end goal with no month-by-month trajectory showing the ramp.
Tier 3 — Nice to Have
F9Cap Table SummaryAbsent
Not discussed. Three cofounders + advisor (Seibel) — what's the equity split? Any prior funding instruments?
F10Revenue AssumptionsPresent
Clear bottom-up model: 2B+ trips worldwide → 560M budget/online → 84M trips with AB&B (15% of SAM) → $25 avg fee → $2.1B at full scale. Near-term: 80K transactions × $25 = $2M in 12 months. The math is shown.
F11Sensitivity AnalysisAbsent
Single scenario only. What if the 15% market capture takes 5 years instead of the implied timeline? What if average booking value is lower?
F12Comparable BenchmarksPartial
Couchsurfing (660K users) and Craigslist (50K weekly listings) cited as market validation. Competition slide maps the landscape well. But no financial benchmarking — what do comparable marketplaces earn per transaction?
