Evaluation Frameworks

Scorecard Method

Bill Payne
Weighted comparative scorecard across seven dimensions, each scored relative to a 100% average. The composite multiplier adjusts comparable startup valuations.

Berkus Method

Dave Berkus
Risk-reduction framework assigning up to $500K per category. Five dimensions map to specific startup risks; max pre-revenue valuation of $2.5M.

 03

Elements of Enduring Companies

Sequoia Capital
Qualitative lens for identifying companies built to last. Evaluates clarity of purpose, market scale ($1B+), customer wealth, singular focus, and whether the product is a painkiller or vitamin.

 04

Jobs to Be Done + Four-Box Model

Clayton Christensen
Tests disruption potential through the customer's job-to-be-done, then stress-tests the business model across four interlocking boxes. Distinguishes low-end from new-market disruption.
